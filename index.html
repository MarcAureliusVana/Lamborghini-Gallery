Ferruccio Lamborghini

He was born in 1916 and developed a keen interest in Mechanics at an early age. It was in 1963 when he started the Lamborghini company.

Tractors

The company still makes Tractors. In 1963, the Lamborghini started as a Tractor manufacturer but later shifted to the automobile business because of Enzo Ferrari.

Unique design

The design of Lamborghini models is inspired by fighter planes. American F-22 and B-2 fighter planes were used as inspiration for designing the modern models.

Models
